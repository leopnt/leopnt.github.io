Research Experience

Oct. 2023

Similarity measure of time-based event sequence data

R&D project at Polytech Nantes focused on maintenance of a Dassault Aviation business jet fleet. The goal was to improve similarity measurement between actual and theoretical maintenance histories and create an interactive visualization to explore differences. Existing methods were adapted and combined to handle temporal events. The proposed solution works but has scalability limitations, leaving room for future optimizations.
